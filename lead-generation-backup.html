How is this different from robo-advisors like Betterment or Wealthfront?

+
Robo-advisors use basic portfolio allocation templates applied to everyone. QuantEdge creates custom algorithms designed specifically for your goals, risk tolerance, and timeline. Our PhD quants build proprietary models that adapt in real-time to market conditions—not static ETF portfolios. Think of robo-advisors as economy class; we're private jet service. Plus, you have direct access to our senior team, not just automated software.

What's the minimum investment to get started?

+
We work with private investors starting at $500,000 in investable assets. This ensures we can properly diversify strategies and cover the computational resources required for custom algorithm development. However, we evaluate each situation individually during the strategy session—if you're close to this threshold and serious about optimizing your portfolio, we're happy to discuss options.

How do you protect against market crashes and extreme volatility?

+
Risk management is at the core of everything we do. Our systems employ: multi-layered hedging strategies that automatically activate during volatility spikes; real-time position sizing that reduces exposure when risk metrics elevate; circuit breakers that halt trading if parameters are breached; and stress testing against 50+ historical crisis scenarios. During the 2020 COVID crash, while markets fell 35%, our algorithms limited client drawdowns to -8.2% on average while capturing the recovery upside.

Can I withdraw my funds anytime, or am I locked in?

+
You maintain full liquidity—this is your money, not ours. You can request withdrawals at any time with 5 business days notice for processing (standard for institutional accounts). There are no lock-up periods or penalties. We're confident in our performance, so we don't need to trap clients. That said, we recommend a minimum 12-month investment horizon for optimal results, as short-term market noise can obscure the statistical edge our algorithms deliver over time.

What if the algorithms stop working? Markets change constantly.

+
This is actually why machine learning is superior to static strategies. Our algorithms continuously adapt and learn from new market data. We also employ ensemble methods—multiple algorithms working simultaneously, so if one model underperforms, others compensate. Plus, our research team constantly develops new factors and strategies. We have human oversight monitoring performance daily and quarterly strategy reviews to ensure alignment. This isn't "set and forget"—it's dynamic, intelligent management that evolves with markets.

Traditional advisors charge 1-2% annually regardless of performance, plus hidden fund fees averaging another 0.5-1.5%.
